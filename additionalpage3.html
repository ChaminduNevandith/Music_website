2.Violin

The violin is one of the most important and versatile instruments in Western classical music, and it is also used in many other genres, including folk, country, jazz, and rock. It is often used to play melody lines, but it can also be used for accompaniment, harmony, and rhythm.The violin is a musical instrument that is part of the string family. 3.Piano.

Pianos are musical instruments that produce sound by striking strings with hammers. They are played by pressing keys on a keyboard, which causes the hammers to strike the strings and produce different notes. Pianos are widely used in a variety of musical genres, including classical, jazz, pop, and rock.

4.Drums.

Drums are musical instruments that produce sound through the vibration of a stretched membrane or skin. They are typically played with sticks or brushes, but can also be played with the hands. Drums are used in a wide range of musical genres, including rock, pop, jazz, blues, and many others.
